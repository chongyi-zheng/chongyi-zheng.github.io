Chongyi Zheng

chongyiz@princeton.edu

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at Princeton University advised by Benjamin Eysenbach. I am interested in developing reinforcement learning (RL) algorithms that enable long-horizon reasoning using probabilistic inference. I completed my M.S. from Carnegie Mellon University advised by Ruslan Salakhutdinov. I have had the great opportunity to collaborate with Sergey Levine and work with Xiaolong Wang.
