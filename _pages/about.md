I am a PhD student in [Computer Science](https://www.cs.princeton.edu/) at [Princeton University](https://www.princeton.edu/) advised by [Benjamin Eysenbach](https://ben-eysenbach.github.io/). I am interested in developing <b>reinforcement learning (RL) algorithms</b> that can leverage prior knowledge, reason over long horizons, and gather information in a unsupervised manner. To this end, I work on unsupervised RL, goal-conditioned RL, and representation learning for control.
